- May reduce our margins significantly

- Negotiate flat annual fee instead of per-user royalty
- Emphasize co-marketing value (reduces our customer acquisition cost)

## üìù Key Takeaways

### What Makes a Strong Sponsor?

1. **Workforce Development Mission**: Organizations that already invest in talent development (ERA, Alberta Innovates consortia)
2. **Funding Authority**: Can write checks or approve grants (ERA, Alberta Innovates)
3. **Clear ROI**: Trained professionals directly support their funded projects/portfolio companies
4. **Strategic Alignment**: Our content addresses their pain points (carbon compliance for ERA, grid modernization for Alberta Innovates)

### Red Flags to Avoid

1. **Mission Mismatch**: Organizations with tangential interest in energy education (CME's manufacturing focus)
2. **Leadership Transitions**: Organizations in flux may delay decisions (Alberta Innovates CEO change)
3. **Competitive Overlap**: Organizations with existing training platforms (CME SkillsPass)
