# AESO Market Rules Basics

## Scope
Short reference notes for Alberta market-rules and dispatch context.

## Core Concepts
- AESO market rules define how Alberta electricity market participation, settlements, and operating conditions are interpreted.
- CEIP explanations should separate operator rules from UI-derived summaries.
- Any market-rule summary should keep the date of the rule or source page visible when possible.

## Why It Matters in CEIP
- Market-rule context helps the LLM explain pool-price behavior without pretending to be a trading desk.
- It is a better grounding layer than generic “real-time dashboard” wording.

## Retrieval Hints
- Relevant entities: AESO market rules, settlement, dispatch, offers, balancing.
- Relevant user intents: market mechanics summary, rule explanation, pool-price context.
