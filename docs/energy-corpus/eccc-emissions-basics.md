# ECCC Emissions Basics

## Scope
Short reference notes for Environment and Climate Change Canada emissions and climate context.

## Core Concepts
- ECCC provides emissions inventory, climate, and weather context used across CEIP analysis flows.
- Emissions narratives should distinguish measured inventory values from modeled or estimated values.
- Weather context should support forecasting and emissions explanations, not replace operator data.

## Why It Matters in CEIP
- Carbon and transition dashboards need a clear explanation of what is measured, what is estimated, and what is stale.
- Future RAG prompts should retrieve source-grounded climate context instead of inventing policy language.

## Retrieval Hints
- Relevant entities: ECCC, emissions inventory, climate projections, greenhouse gases, weather context.
- Relevant user intents: emissions summary, climate policy context, carbon-intensity explanation, weather context for forecasts.
