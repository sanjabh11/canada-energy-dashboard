# Indigenous Energy Basics

## Scope
Short reference notes for Indigenous clean-energy and sovereignty context used by CEIP.

## Core Concepts
- Indigenous energy work should be framed around sovereignty, partnership, and community priorities.
- Product copy should avoid generic “impact” language that erases local governance or OCAP-style concerns.
- Funding and project narratives should stay source-anchored and not imply universal eligibility.

## Why It Matters in CEIP
- Indigenous project flows and consultation narratives need respectful, source-aware framing.
- Future RAG prompts should ground community and funding context before generating recommendations.

## Retrieval Hints
- Relevant entities: Indigenous energy, clean energy, consultation, partnership, funding.
- Relevant user intents: project context, funding overview, partnership summary, consultation framing.
