# CEIP Canada Outreach and Immigration Positioning Matrix

## Objective

Use the Canada Energy Intelligence Platform (CEIP) as a credibility asset for:

1. Energy consulting and enterprise sales
2. Teaching, cohort training, and invited-expert engagements
3. Immigration-adjacent business visitor, employer-sponsored, and partner-led opportunities in Canada

This brief is designed to be handed to Perplexity Computer for target discovery and campaign execution.

## What The Website Can Realistically Do

The repo already contains the right surfaces for three distinct uses:

- **Enterprise / municipal / training outreach** through dedicated landing pages and campaign capture.
- **Proof-of-expertise positioning** through sector dashboards, especially energy, compliance, hydrogen, minerals, ESG, and stakeholder coordination.
- **Canada-focused credibility** through a resume asset and Alberta-specific messaging around Calgary, LMIA, Global Talent Stream, and municipal / Indigenous / utility use cases.

This is best used as a:

- consulting credibility engine
- teaching / cohort enrollment engine
- employer / partner conversation starter
- invited-expert / business-visitor support asset

It should **not** be positioned as a direct “entrepreneur PR shortcut.” Current federal entrepreneur/self-employed pathways are narrow or paused.

## Current Official Immigration Reality

Use the following current official pages as guardrails:

- [Start-up Visa Program](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/start-visa.html) — open only to legacy commitment-certificate cases; closed to new applications.
- [Self-Employed Persons Program](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/self-employed.html) — paused.
- [Business visitors attending meetings, events and conferences in Canada](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/visit-canada/business/visitors-events-conferences.html) — relevant for short visits, meetings, conferences, and events.
- [Hire a foreign academic – Exemptions](https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/services/foreign-workers/academic/exemption.html) — relevant for guest lecturers, visiting professors, academic consultants, and related academic exceptions.
- [Rural Entrepreneur Stream](https://www.alberta.ca/aaip-rural-entrepreneur-stream) — an Alberta entrepreneurial route, but only as a niche partner-led business track.
- [Foreign Graduate Entrepreneur Stream](https://www.alberta.ca/aaip-foreign-graduate-entrepreneur-stream-eligibility) — only for eligible foreign-educated graduates meeting AAIP requirements.
