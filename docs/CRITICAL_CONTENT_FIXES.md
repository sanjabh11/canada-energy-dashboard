## TIER Question 2 (REPLACE)

**Question:**
```
Under the 2025 TIER amendments, what is the formula for calculating Direct Investment Credits (DIC)?
```

**Options:**
```
A. IC = Eligible Investment × $95
B. IC = Eligible Investment ÷ $95  ← CORRECT
C. IC = Eligible Investment + $95
D. IC = Eligible Investment - $95
```

**Correct Answer:** B

**Explanation:**
```
Investment Credits = Eligible Investment ÷ $95 (the TIER fund price). Example: $9.5 million investment ÷ $95 = 100,000 credits.
```
