Invoke the `fable5-with-outreach` skill to perform a marketing strategy and
full product lifecycle audit of this repository.

The skill is located at `~/.codeium/windsurf/skills/fable5-with-outreach/SKILL.md`.

Read the SKILL.md file and follow its instructions to:

1. **Phase M0**: Calibrate to the project's commercial infrastructure
2. **Phase M1**: Marketing strategy & positioning audit (April Dunford framework, messaging hierarchy, claim boundaries)
3. **Phase M2**: Outreach & engagement strategy audit (cadence, compliance, dark funnel)
4. **Phase M3**: Pricing & monetization strategy audit (value metric, tier design, price-to-value)
5. **Phase M4**: Buyer journey & touchpoint audit (6 stages, touchpoint matrix, drop-off analysis)
6. **Phase M5**: Sales enablement artifact audit (10 artifact types)
7. **Phase M6**: GTM readiness gate (8-dimension scorecard)
8. **Phase M7**: Customer validation & presales lifecycle audit (maturity scoring, 10-stage funnel, pipeline velocity)
9. **Phase M8**: Competitive intelligence matrix (9 dimensions × 3+ competitors)
10. **Phase M9**: Improvement plan with agent-executable tasks (M0-M4 milestones)

Output: `docs/audits/outreach-audit-{YYYY-MM-DD}.md`

Optional focus (pass as argument):
- No argument: full marketing/outreach/presales lifecycle audit
- `positioning`: positioning statement and messaging hierarchy only
- `outreach`: outreach cadence, channel mix, and compliance only
- `pricing`: pricing strategy and monetization model only
- `buyer-journey`: buyer journey mapping and touchpoint audit only
- `sales-enablement`: sales enablement artifact audit only
- `gtm-readiness`: GTM readiness gate only
- `customer-validation`: customer discovery and validation evidence only
- `presales`: presales lifecycle and conversion funnel only
- `competitive`: competitive intelligence deep dive only
