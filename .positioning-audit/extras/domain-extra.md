## Domain-Specific Evaluation Criteria

### TIER Compliance Software Evaluation
- Fund price provenance: Must cite Canada-Alberta Implementation Agreement
- Market credit price: Must be labeled as fallback if not live
- DI pathway: Must be labeled as estimated until standard releases
- Claim boundaries: Must not claim guaranteed savings, live market price, broker execution, or tax advice
- Reporting deadline: Annual compliance reports due June 30

### Ontario Utility Planning Evaluation
- OEB Chapter 5 alignment: Planning assumptions must be defensible
- GA/ICI 5CP: Class A customers, 5 coincident peak hours, annual adjustment period
- Rate case cycle: Typically 3-5 years between filings

### Municipal Energy Evaluation
- NWPTA threshold: Sub-$75K can use procurement vehicle (Canoe)
- MCCAC funding: Municipal Climate Change Action Centre programs
- FCM Green Municipal Fund: Funding source for municipal energy projects
